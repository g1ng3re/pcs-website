Why We Run Events

The property knowledge gap is costing people money.

Mortgages, credit, protection, and investment are not taught in school. We run events to close that gap, openly and in public, for anyone who wants to learn. Most of the costly mistakes we see in advice are not bad luck. They are unexplained decisions. Our events exist to make sure ours do not stay that way.

Real community

Connect with people on the same journey. First-time buyers, investors, professionals. The room is the room.

Real professionals

Every session is led by working professionals from the property and finance sector. Practical, current, and field-tested. Not theory.

Real tools

Every attendee leaves with checklists, guides, and frameworks they can actually apply. Calculators, document lists, and decision trees.

Common Questions

Event FAQs.

Are the events sales pitches in disguise?

No. The events are education led. Our advisers are usually in the room, available to answer questions and book follow-ups for those who want one. The teaching stays the teaching.

Do I need any prior knowledge to attend?

No. Sessions are designed to work for complete beginners and to still be useful for experienced clients. Our team can point you to the right session if you are unsure.

Are events online or in person?

Both. Most workshops are in person across the UK. Some sessions are online, particularly for clients who cannot travel.

How do I get notified of new events?

Join the newsletter. One email a month with upcoming events, market updates, and useful resources.

Can my company or church host a session?

Yes. We run private community events for employers, churches, and networks. Submit a request and we will get back to you within one working day.

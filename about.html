Our Story

Built on purpose. Driven by impact.

Property Clinic Solutions was founded to make property and finance simple, accessible, and stress free. Our qualified specialists provide independent guidance and practical solutions built around each client's situation, not a standard template.

We support homeowners, landlords, investors, and tenants across mortgages, protection, and property education. Every recommendation is built on transparency, professionalism, and a commitment to doing what is right for the client.

Our mission is straightforward: help individuals, families, and businesses achieve their property goals with clarity, confidence, and long-term support. We are not here for one transaction. We are here to build lasting relationships.

The Team

Meet Emmanuel.

CeMAP QualifiedFCA Regulated15+ Years Experience

Emmanuel Olukotun

Founder and Director. CeMAP qualified.

Growing up in Brixton in the early 90s, Emmanuel saw firsthand how money operates as a tool of power, and how the lack of financial literacy quietly damages individuals, families, and entire communities.

With more than 15 years across the public and private sectors, including three London local authorities, Emmanuel is now a CeMAP qualified mortgage and protection adviser and Director of Blackstone Property Group.

His focus is teaching people the principles of property and protection, and showing how strategic decisions can change the trajectory of a family. He is a strong advocate of ownership and regularly encourages social tenants to consider the path to becoming homeowners.

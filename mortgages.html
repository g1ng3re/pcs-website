Common Questions

Mortgage questions, answered straight.

How much can I borrow?

Most lenders work on around 4.5 times your income, but stretch lenders and specialist criteria can push that higher in the right circumstances. We will run the numbers across multiple lenders before recommending anything.

How much deposit do I need?

From 5% for residential, typically from 25% for buy-to-let. Larger deposits open better rates, but we will tell you the lowest viable deposit for your case.

Do you charge a fee?

We charge a fee only on completion. There is no upfront cost to find out whether we can help.

What if I have adverse credit?

We work with specialist lenders who consider defaults, CCJs, missed payments, and IVAs. The right lender depends on what happened and when. We will tell you honestly what is realistic.

How long does it take?

From application to offer is typically 2 to 4 weeks. From offer to completion depends on the chain and your solicitor. We push the process at every stage.

Will applying hurt my credit score?

Decisions in principle usually use a soft search. A full application is a hard search. We sequence things to avoid unnecessary hits to your credit file.
